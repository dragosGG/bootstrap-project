About Dragos

Dragos started his comedy career in the Lion City: Singapore. He has performed across Asia and Europe, in places such as Bangkok, Hong Kong, Kuala Lumpur, Tokyo, Berlin, Barcelona, Tallin and London.

Style of Comedy

Best way to sum up Dragos's style is "cute story telling". Whether it's recounting the experiences of living in the United Kingdom or recounting memories of interacting with cultures across Asia, Dragos highlights his stories in a somewhat innocent but hilarious manner.

Let there be Jokes!

Dragos tours regularly around Europe with planned stints in Asia. If you are keen on having a good laugh and want to learn more about how the world looks like through the eyes of comedian, please do come by and say hi at a show. He doesn’t bite and has had all his vaccinations done… I think.
